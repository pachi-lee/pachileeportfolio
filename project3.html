Health Research

Technical Skills learned:
  • R-studio
  • Fischer exact test
  • Multivariable logistic regression Test

The goal of this project was to collect data in the community about the knowledge gap of tick vector borne disease in the Hmong community. My part as a research assistant was to collect, analyze primary research data, conduct over 300 survey interviews, and document results according to established policies and procedures.

I provided technical expertise to fundamental and applied scientific research under the direction of senior research personnel Xia Lee.

We used R-Studio to create analytical data, define variables, use methods like fisher exact test, and multivariable logistic regression to compare variables, and organized and cleaned missing data.
